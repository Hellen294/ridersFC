Akeem Akinola - Member

Director of Operations and Facilities

  • Oversee and manage the club's daily operations to ensure smooth functioning.
  • Ensure all logistical arrangements for training sessions and matches are efficiently handled.
  • Coordinate with other directors to implement and uphold club policies and decisions.
  • Ensure all training and match facilities are well-maintained and in top condition.
  • Oversee the procurement and maintenance of all equipment and kits.
  • Manage the setup and breakdown of equipment for training sessions and matches.
